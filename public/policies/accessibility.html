Last reviewed: September 1, 2025

A Place Called Home LLC is committed to digital accessibility. We strive to conform to the WCAG 2.2 Level AA guidelines and to provide a site usable by people with disabilities.

Measures We Support

  • Keyboard‑accessible navigation and focus styles.
  • Semantic landmarks and headings.
  • Text alternatives for images and icons.
  • Reduced motion support (prefers-reduced-motion).
  • Color contrast review and ongoing testing.

Compatibility

Our website works with current versions of major browsers on desktop and mobile operating systems. Some older browsers may not fully support modern accessibility features.

Continuous Improvement

We regularly audit this site and welcome suggestions. Our goal is to deliver an inclusive experience to all visitors.
