This Privacy Policy explains how A Place Called Home LLC ("APCH", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you visit apchllc.com or contact us. We comply with applicable Texas and U.S. privacy laws, including the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA).

Information We Collect

  • Contact data you provide: name, email, phone, preferred contact method/time, and message details via our forms.
  • Tour request submissions: details you share to schedule a visit.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser information, pages viewed, and timestamps (standard server logs).
  • Security/anti‑spam: hCaptcha responses and related signals when enabled.
  • Cookies/Storage: limited usage to improve site function; we do not track for ad targeting.

Do not submit protected health information (PHI) or sensitive medical details on our public website forms. Our site is informational and not a medical portal under HIPAA. We can discuss care needs privately by phone or during a tour.

How We Use Information

  • Respond to inquiries and schedule tours.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the website.
  • Send confirmations and transactional notices (e.g., tour details).
  • Limited updates about our services (you may opt out at any time).
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud/abuse.

Sharing and Disclosure

  • Service providers that host our site, process forms, or send email on our behalf (e.g., Supabase, email providers). They may only use data to provide contracted services.
  • Legal if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and property.
  • We do not sell personal information.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Opt‑out of non‑essential emails using the link provided or by contacting us.
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your information by emailing support@apchllc.com.
  • Browser controls to manage cookies and local storage.

Data Security & Retention

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate for a small assisted‑living provider. We retain contact records only as long as necessary for operational and legal purposes.

Children

Our website is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

International Visitors

Our website and services are intended for use in the United States. By using our site you understand your data is processed in the U.S.

Changes
